Our founding

Purdue Launch was founded in March 2023 with the goal to provide practical, pro-bono consulting experience in a variety of fields for our small member base. We chose a small member base so we could provide as much experience as possible for our members while also maintaining personal relationships within the club and our clients.

Our mission

We have a mission to create a platform for high-achieving Purdue students to express their interest in consulting and the entrepreneurial space. By working with emerging startups, Purdue Launch aims to advance students' practical skills while offering an opportunity to collaborate with unique and innovative professionals.
